INVOICE FORMAT THAT WORKS WITH RECONCILEAI
===========================================

Required Fields:
1. Invoice Number: Must have "Invoice #:" or "Invoice Number:" or "PO Number:"
2. Vendor Name: Must have "Vendor:" label
3. Invoice Date: Must have "Date:" or "Invoice Date:" or "PO Date:" in format YYYY-MM-DD or MM/DD/YYYY
4. Total Amount: Must have "Total:" or "Total Authorized Amount:" with $ sign
5. Line Items: Must be in table format with columns: Description | Quantity | Unit Price | Total

SAMPLE INVOICE TEXT FORMAT:
---------------------------

INVOICE

Vendor: Office Supplies Co.
Invoice Number: INV-12345
Date: 2026-03-07

Item Description                    Quantity    Unit Price    Total
Office Chairs                       5           $150          $750
Desk Lamps                         10           $25           $250
Printer Paper (Reams)              20           $8            $160

Subtotal: $1160
Tax: $116
Total: $1276

PURCHASE ORDER

Vendor: Tech Equipment Inc.

Item Description                    Quantity    Unit Price    Total
Laptops (Dell XPS)                 3           $1200         $3600
Monitors (27 inch)                 6           $300          $1800
Keyboards                          10          $50           $500

Subtotal: $5900
Shipping: $100
Tax: $600
Total Authorized Amount: $6600


KEY FORMATTING RULES:
---------------------
1. Use clear labels: "Vendor:", "Invoice Number:", "Date:", "Total:"
2. Line items must have 4 columns: Description, Quantity, Unit Price, Total
3. Use $ signs for prices
4. Separate columns with spaces (at least 2-3 spaces)
5. Keep descriptions simple (avoid special characters)
6. Use whole numbers or decimals with 2 places (e.g., $150 or $150.00)
